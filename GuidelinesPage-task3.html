Guidelines

How to Play

The objective of Tic Tac Toe is to be the first player to get three of your marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally). The game is played on a 3x3 grid.

  • Player X always goes first.
  • Players alternate placing their X or O marks in empty cells.
  • The first player to get 3 marks in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) is the winner.
  • If all 9 cells are filled and neither player has 3 in a row, the game is a draw.

Playing Options

You can play Tic Tac Toe against:

  • Another player (friend)
  • An AI opponent

NOTE:Choose your opponent type using the options provided in the game interface/Home page(Do scroll down to choose)
